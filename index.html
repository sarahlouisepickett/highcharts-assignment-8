U.S Foreign Aid Spending

By Sarah L. Pickett

The United States has been tracking US aid spending from since 1946. This chart looks at total obligations from the data from 2010 to 2017. Obligations refer to money that will be disbursed. In 2017, the disbursments were lower than years past.

The U.S Aid Explorer divides spending up by sector and then by implementing agency, which means the agency that spends the money. This second chart shows spending by sector. The sector with the most spending in 2017 was Conflict, Peace and Security.
